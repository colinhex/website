"Why does my code not compile?"

When I joined the Computer Science faculty in Basel as an undergraduate, I had never written a single line of code. What I learned very quickly was that no matter the subject: some basic understanding, engagement and interest can make a fool be a rookie, and let a rookie become savvy.

Through the course of my degree I was dabbling first in Java, then MatLab and Python, then some C++ and SQL and, at the time of writing, in the basics of web-development. The biggest "Ahaa"-moment was not some deeply-buried-in-the-stack exception, though there were quite a few of those, but the sheer size of all individual and interconnected software-systems that span the globe and drive the economy, society and politics of today and tomorrow.

So, what is my place in the world of tomorrow? I think it's here!.
